# Flavor Pairing Profiles for Cookie Design

This reference provides detailed flavor compatibility information for cookie creation.

## Chocolate Pairings

**Dark Chocolate** pairs well with:
- Orange, raspberry, cherry
- Coffee, espresso
- Mint, peppermint
- Sea salt, caramel
- Chili, cayenne
- Nuts: hazelnuts, almonds

**Milk Chocolate** pairs well with:
- Peanut butter
- Caramel, toffee
- Vanilla, cream
- Banana
- Pecans, walnuts

**White Chocolate** pairs well with:
- Lemon, lime, passion fruit
- Raspberry, strawberry
- Lavender
- Matcha, green tea
- Macadamia, pistachio
- Cranberry

## Fruit Pairings

**Citrus** (lemon, orange, lime):
- Berries (especially blueberry, raspberry)
- Lavender, rosemary, thyme
- Poppy seeds
- White chocolate
- Almond

**Stone Fruits** (cherry, peach, apricot):
- Almond, amaretto
- Vanilla
- Cardamom, cinnamon
- Dark chocolate
- Honey

**Berries**:
- Lemon, lime
- Cream cheese, mascarpone
- White chocolate
- Vanilla
- Basil, mint

## Spice Combinations

**Warming Spices**:
- Cinnamon + nutmeg + clove (classic blend)
- Cardamom + vanilla + honey
- Ginger + molasses + brown sugar
- Chai spice blend: cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, cloves, black pepper

**Unexpected Spice Pairings**:
- Black pepper + strawberry
- Rosemary + lemon
- Lavender + honey + lemon
- Chili + chocolate + cinnamon

## Nut and Seed Pairings

- Almond: cherry, chocolate, orange, cardamom
- Hazelnut: chocolate, coffee, caramel, banana
- Pecan: caramel, maple, bourbon, brown butter
- Pistachio: rose water, cardamom, white chocolate, lemon
- Walnut: maple, brown sugar, banana, coffee
- Sesame: honey, ginger, chocolate, miso

## Texture Elements

**Crunchy**:
- Toffee bits, brittle
- Toasted nuts
- Crystallized ginger
- Cereal pieces
- Cookie crumbs

**Chewy**:
- Dried fruits (cranberries, cherries, apricots)
- Caramel pieces
- Marshmallow
- Coconut flakes

**Smooth**:
- Chocolate ganache
- Caramel filling
- Cream cheese
- Nut butters

## Seasonal Flavor Guides

**Spring**:
- Lemon, strawberry, lavender
- Earl Grey tea, honey
- Rhubarb, vanilla
- Floral notes (rose, orange blossom)

**Summer**:
- Tropical fruits (mango, pineapple, coconut)
- Berries, stone fruits
- Citrus, mint
- Light and refreshing combinations

**Fall**:
- Apple, pumpkin, pear
- Maple, brown butter
- Cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger
- Caramel, pecan

**Winter**:
- Peppermint, chocolate
- Gingerbread spices
- Cranberry, orange
- Coffee, eggnog flavors
